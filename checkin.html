THEATER/TVS

There are a lot of TVs/remotes in the house, but the TV in the home theater has the most moving parts. The home theater has a cable box, BluRay player, and XBox controlled by the receiver, a big black box in the console underneath the screen called a receiver.








 

To use the device of choice:

1. The receiver must be on and set to the right input 
2. The projector must be on
3. The device you want to be used must be on.

To turn on any device, just match the brand of the remote to the brand of the device. For receiver inputs:

SAT/CATV = Cable TV 
BD/DVD = BluRay
GAME = XBox
